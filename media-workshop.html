Media workshop

Detailed workshops organized for students across the past year.

Each workshop is designed around a specific production skill, with practice time, small group tasks, and final sharing so members can apply what they learn immediately.

18-19 March 2026 | 2 days

Campus Lens: Photography Bootcamp

A beginner-friendly workshop led by the club media team. Day 1 covered camera settings, exposure, composition, and campus portrait basics. Day 2 focused on event photography practice around campus with feedback from senior members.

  • Outcome: members submitted a 10-photo campus story.
  • Skills: framing, lighting, movement shots, photo selection.
10 April 2026 | 1 day

Reels That Tell Stories

This short-form video workshop taught students how to plan, shoot, and edit a 30-second reel for campus events. Members learned shot lists, pacing, caption placement, music timing, and export settings for Instagram and TikTok.

  • Outcome: each group created one event recap reel.
  • Skills: storyboarding, phone videography, editing rhythm.
24-26 May 2026 | 3 days

Podcast Studio Starter Lab

A practical podcast workshop for hosts, producers, and editors. Students learned episode research, guest briefing, mic technique, room setup, audio cleanup, and how to structure a campus conversation into a complete episode.

  • Outcome: teams recorded a 6-minute pilot episode.
  • Skills: hosting, scripting, audio levels, interview flow.
