This page explains how basic contact information may be handled.

Information we may receive

If visitors use a contact or joining form, they may provide a name, email address, and message. This information should only be used by the club team to respond to enquiries, membership interest, or collaboration requests.

  • Information should not be sold or shared for unrelated marketing.
  • Messages may be reviewed by relevant committee members.
  • Students may request correction or removal of submitted details by contacting the club.
