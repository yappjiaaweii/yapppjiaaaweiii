About the club

A student-led space for media practice, teamwork, and creative confidence.

INTI Mass Comm Club supports students who want to explore journalism, production, photography, videography, podcasting, social media, and event communication beyond the classroom.

Mission

To help students build practical media skills through workshops, real event coverage, and collaborative creative projects.

Vision

To become a trusted student media hub that represents INTI campus life with creativity, accuracy, and professionalism.

Community

Members learn by doing: planning shoots, interviewing guests, editing content, and supporting each other as a production team.
