Club background

From a small student idea to a confident media community.

INTI Mass Comm Club was formed in 2023 by students who wanted a practical platform for photography, videography, podcasting, event coverage, and storytelling on campus.

How the club began

The club began in 2023 under the first president, Alex Leo, with a simple aim: give Mass Communication students a place to practise real media work before stepping into internships or client projects. The early committee started by covering small campus events, helping students learn camera basics, caption writing, and simple editing workflows.

As more students joined, the club expanded into structured workshops, event coverage teams, podcast production, and creative collaborations. Members were encouraged to rotate roles, so a student could try photography one month, production planning the next, and social media editing after that.

  • 2023: The first committee formed the club identity and basic production team structure.
  • 2024: Members began supporting campus societies with event photo and video coverage.
  • 2025: The club introduced podcast and short-form video projects for wider storytelling practice.
  • 2026: The club continues to grow as a modern student media team built on creativity, discipline, and teamwork.

Today, INTI Mass Comm Club is more than a society name. It is a place where students learn to speak with images, sound, words, and honest collaboration, turning campus moments into stories worth remembering.
