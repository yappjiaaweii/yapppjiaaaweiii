Media projects

Named projects, dates, formats, and collaborations.

These project examples show how the club turns photography, videography, audio, and external collaborations into portfolio-ready student work.

January 2026 | Photography series

Faces of INTI: Student Portrait Series

A portrait photography project featuring students from different faculties. The team handled concept planning, portrait sessions, short captions, and final gallery selection.

February 2026 | Short film

After Class

A 5-minute short film about friendship and deadline pressure. Members worked as director, camera crew, sound recordist, editor, and poster designer.

18 March 2026 | Outside project with MY FM

MY FM Campus Radio Visit

The club collaborated with MY FM for a student media visit, producing behind-the-scenes photos, interview snippets, and a reflection reel about radio production.

5-7 January 2026 | Outside project with ASTRO

ASTRO New Year MV Shooting Support

Selected club members supported an external New Year music video shoot as production assistants, behind-the-scenes photographers, and social media content helpers.

April 2026 | Podcast production

Campus Voices: Student Creative Life

A conversational podcast project featuring student hosts and guests discussing media assignments, creative blocks, internships, and campus stories.

June 2026 | Social media campaign

INTI Fest Countdown Campaign

A countdown content package with teaser reels, event posters, committee portraits, and post-event recap assets prepared for a campus festival team.
