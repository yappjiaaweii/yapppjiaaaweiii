Mission & Vision

Established to bridge the gap between classroom theory and industry practice, the INTI Mass Comm Club has evolved into a vibrant ecosystem for aspiring creators, filmmakers, and digital marketers.

Our Mission

To empower students with practical media industry skills while fostering a community of creative storytellers and technical innovators at INTI.

Our Vision

To be a leading student-led media hub at INTI, nurturing a culture of journalistic integrity, creative excellence, and professional media standards.

Club Background

We are a community of creators and storytellers at INTI who love media. Here, we help each other grow, work on real projects, and learn what it takes to make an impact in the digital world together.
